Chess for everyone everywhere

Our Mission

Here at Chess Connection we strive to bring chess to everyone and anyone in the world. No matter what age or background chess can be a great hobby - not to mention all the benefits it brings. Chess can help improve critical thinking skills, planning skills, problem solving skills, and helps with performing in tough situations. Also, it can be super fun! Chess Connection does not just look to give lessons and help you play chess at a higher level, we want you to be part of our chess community and the chess community that spans worldwide. Come by for a complimentary lesson or leave your email in the box above to inquire about memberships and ask questions about the features listed below.

Online Lessons

We have over a dozen professional chess coaches with ratings from 1800 to Grandmaster ready and willing to help you improve your game at your own pace.

In-House Tournaments

Chess connection hosts in-house tournements every Friday which can be attended either in person or online - win prizes like chess sets, Chess Connection merch, and more!

Community Connections

By becoming a member of Chess Connection and participating in tournaments you can make connections with local and worldwide community members who share a love for chess.
